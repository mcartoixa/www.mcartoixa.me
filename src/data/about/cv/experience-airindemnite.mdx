I helped Air Indemnité transition to a remote only organization by defining a new culture based around asynchronous, continuous and written communication. I also set up the tools supporting it (<IconText name="devicon:slack">_Slack_</IconText>, <IconText name="logos:google-drive">_Google Drive_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:confluence">_Confluence_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:jira">_JIRA_</IconText>). I managed a small team of developers in a tailor-made brand of Agile Methodology with mob programming, unit testing and continuous integration (and continuous delivery).

I moved our web infrastructure (<IconText name="devicon:debian">_Debian_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:nginx">_nginx_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:mariadb">_MariaDB_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:rails-wordmark">_Ruby on Rails_</IconText>) to Microsoft Azure (<IconText name="devicon:azure">_Azure Container Apps_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:azure">_Azure Front Door_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:docker">_Docker_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:mysql">_MySQL_</IconText>) to improve costs, reliability and ease of deployment. I also set up Continuous Integration on all our projects (_Rake_, <IconText name="devicon:githubactions">_Github Actions_</IconText>), including for our Salesforce backend (_Apache Ant_, <IconText name="devicon:salesforce">_Salesforce CLI_</IconText>). I developed a new version of our main customer form in a leaner, more efficient and better tested fashion (<IconText name="devicon:vuejs">_Vue.js_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:nodejs">_node.js_</IconText>). I modelled and prototyped a new version of our information system (<IconText name="devicon:dotnetcore">_.NET Core_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:nodejs">_node.js_</IconText>, _event sourcing_, _CQRS_) and of our web infrastructure (<IconText name="devicon:astro">_Astro_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:nodejs">_node.js_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:vuejs">_Vue.js_</IconText>).

I also handled the IT infrastructure for the company, in a hybrid fashion at first (_DHCP_, _DNS_, _Synology_, <IconText name="devicon:windows8">_Windows Server 2012_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:windows8">_Windows 10_</IconText>) and then in the cloud (<IconText name="devicon:google">_Google Workspace_</IconText>, <IconText name="devicon:azure">_Microsoft Entra_</IconText>).
